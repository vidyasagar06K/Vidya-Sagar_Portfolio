MyExperience

Research Internship

Location : National Institute of Technology (NIT), Warangal

Duration : Dec 2023 - Jan 2024

Role : Research Intern

Project Title : "Artificial Intelligence-Based Smart Knee Brace"

Project Overview : The AI-Based Smart Knee Brace is a healthcare innovation designed to monitor knee joint movement in real time, aiding in rehabilitation and post-surgery recovery. It uses a Raspberry Pi 4 and MPU6050 gyroscope sensor to measure the knee’s flexion and extension angles, tracking a range of motion from 0° to 135°–140°. The system processes data using Python, periodically logs it into Excel, and sends it via email for remote monitoring. An AI model (Random Forest Algorithm) analyzes the data to classify the knee condition as normal or abnormal. This was an individual project, and I was solely responsible for both the hardware integration and the AI-based classification system.
